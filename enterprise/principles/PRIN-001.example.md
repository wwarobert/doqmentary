---
id: PRIN-001
title: Prefer managed services over self-hosted
status: active
tags: [operations, cost, reliability]
---

**Statement.** Prefer managed/platform services over self-hosted equivalents unless a
documented requirement makes self-hosting necessary.

**Rationale.** Managed services shift undifferentiated operational load (patching,
scaling, backups, availability) to the platform, reducing total cost of ownership and
operational risk.

**Implications.** Self-hosting a capability requires an explicit, recorded
justification (typically captured as a decision in the decisions library).

## Context
Teams were deploying containers to a mix of self-managed orchestrators, creating
inconsistent operations, duplicated tooling, and uneven security posture.

## Decision
All containerized workloads run on the organization's managed container platform
unless an approved exception exists.

## Consequences
- Consistent operations, scaling, and patching across teams (supports `PRIN-001`).
- Self-managed orchestrators require a documented exception.
- Some niche workloads may need adaptation to fit the platform's constraints.

## Context
Per-application identity stores fragmented access control and made least-privilege and
auditing hard to enforce consistently.

## Decision
All workloads authenticate and authorize through the central identity provider using
workload identities; no application maintains its own credential store.

## Consequences
- Uniform least-privilege and auditing (supports `PRIN-002`).
- Applications must integrate with the central provider before go-live.
- A dependency on the identity provider's availability is introduced and must be
  reflected in each solution's dependency section.
